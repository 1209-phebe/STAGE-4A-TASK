# MealMind — Smart Meal Planner
Ever stared at your kitchen and thought, “I don’t even know what to cook”? MealMind fixes that.

It’s your personal meal assistant that suggests creative meals based on what is **already in your kitchen**
Just list your ingredients or snap a photo — MealMind shows you recipes that fit your mood, health goals, and time.

### Features
Suggests meals from ingredients you already have.
Recognizes pantry items through photos.
Adapts meals to your goals (fitness, weight loss, etc.).
Adjusts recipes to your mood — lazy day? spicy mood? we got you.
Learns from your behavior to make smarter suggestions over time.

### Why MealMind?
Because deciding what to eat shouldn’t feel like solving a math problem.  
MealMind saves you time, money, and energy — and makes cooking fun again.

### Getting Started
Open MealMind.
Add ingredients manually or snap your pantry.
Get meal ideas instantly.
Cook, enjoy, and save your favorites.
